Homework Assignments

To help you master discussing and writing about technology and society:

  1. Speaking Practice:
  • Discuss with a family member or friend how a particular technology (e.g., smartphones, streaming services) has changed daily life for your generation compared to your parents' generation.
  • Try to use at least 5 new words or phrases related to technology and society, highlighting both benefits and drawbacks.
  1. Vocabulary Journal:
  • Review your notebook. Choose your top 5 favorite new words from today and write one original sentence for each, related to technology.
  • Try to make at least two of these sentences use structures for expressing advantages/disadvantages or cause/effect.
  1. Complete the Expository Essay:
  • Finish writing your short expository essay on a technological advancement (aim for about 250-350 words).
  • Focus on clear explanations, balanced perspectives, and include at least 10-12 of the new technology terms and impact-related phrases.
  1. Proofread & Edit:
  • Carefully proofread your essay for grammar, spelling, punctuation, and clarity.
  • Read it aloud to catch awkward phrasing or errors.
  1. Tech Observation:
  • Choose one new piece of technology or a new feature of an existing technology you encounter this week.
  • In your notebook, briefly describe its function, what problem it solves, and what potential ethical implications or drawbacks it might have.

Remember your notebook! Continue to use it actively. Write down new words, good sample sentences, and any tips that help you improve your speaking and writing. This will be a valuable resource for your test!

Lesson 8: Historical Events and Their Impact

Hello students! In this lesson, we're going to explore historical events and their impact. This means learning how to discuss significant moments from the past, understand their causes and consequences, and analyze their lasting effects on society. By the end of this lesson, you'll be able to talk about historical events with greater depth and write concise summaries of their importance.
