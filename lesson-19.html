Homework Assignments

To help you master discussing and writing about health and well-being:

  1. Speaking Practice:
  • Discuss a personal healthy habit you practice (e.g., morning exercise, healthy breakfast, meditation) with a family member or friend.
  • Explain why you do it and its benefits for your physical or mental health, using at least 5 new words or phrases from this lesson.
  1. Vocabulary Journal:
  • Review your notebook. Choose your top 5 favorite new words from today and write one original sentence for each, related to health.
  • Try to make at least two of these sentences use structures for expressing purpose/benefit or giving advice.
  1. Complete the Informative Article:
  • Finish writing your short informative article on a health topic (aim for about 250-350 words).
  • Focus on clear explanations, practical tips, and include at least 10-12 of the new health terms and explanatory phrases.
  1. Proofread & Edit:
  • Carefully proofread your article for grammar, spelling, punctuation, and clarity.
  • Read it aloud to catch awkward phrasing or errors.
  1. Health Habit Tracker:
  • For one week, choose one new healthy habit you want to try (e.g., drinking more water, 15 minutes of stretching, 5 minutes of mindfulness).
  • In your notebook, track your progress daily and briefly note any changes you observe in your well-being.

Remember your notebook! Continue to use it actively. Write down new words, good sample sentences, and any tips that help you improve your speaking and writing. This will be a valuable resource for your test!

Lesson 7: Technology and Society

Hello students! In this lesson, we're going to explore the dynamic relationship between technology and society. This means discussing how technological advancements impact our daily lives, communication, education, and the world at large. By the end of this lesson, you'll be able to debate the pros and cons of technology and write expository essays on technological advancements.
