Part 2: Writing About Technology

Your Writing Guides

When writing about technology and society, your writing should be:

  1. Expository: Explain and inform about a topic.
  2. Balanced: Present both positive and negative aspects (if applicable).
  3. Clear and Logical: Organize information in a way that is easy to follow.
  4. Objective: Present facts and analysis without excessive personal bias.

Writing Structure: The Expository Essay on a Technological Advancement

This structure will help you write a clear and informative expository essay:

  • Title: Clear and descriptive (e.g., "The Transformative Power of Artificial Intelligence").
  • Introduction (1 paragraph):
  • Hook: Start with a general statement about the rapid pace of technological change.
  • Background: Briefly introduce the specific technological advancement you will discuss.
  • Thesis Statement: State the main purpose of your essay – what aspects of this technology's impact will you explore?
  • Example Thesis: "Artificial Intelligence (AI) represents a profound technological innovation that is transforming various sectors of society, offering unprecedented benefits while also posing significant ethical implications."
  • Body Paragraph 1: Positive Impacts/Benefits (1 paragraph):
  • Topic Sentence: Focus on the advantages or positive impacts of the technology.
  • Elaboration: Provide specific examples of how the technology has improved efficiency, accessibility, or other aspects of life.
  • Example: "One of the primary benefits of AI is its ability to enhance efficiency across industries, from automation in manufacturing to personalized learning in education. This innovation facilitates faster problem-solving and greater productivity."
  • Body Paragraph 2: Challenges/Drawbacks/Ethical Concerns (1 paragraph):
  • Topic Sentence: Discuss the challenges, drawbacks, or ethical concerns associated with the technology.
  • Elaboration: Explain potential negative consequences, such as job disruption, data privacy issues, or the need for robust cybersecurity.
  • Example: "However, a major drawback of widespread AI adoption is the potential for job disruption in certain sectors. Furthermore, concerns regarding data privacy and the ethical implications of algorithms must be carefully addressed."
  • Conclusion (1 paragraph):
  • Summarize: Briefly recap the main positive and negative impacts discussed.
  • Reiterate Importance: Emphasize the overall significance of understanding this technology.
  • Final Thought: Offer a forward-looking statement about the future of this technology or the need for responsible development.

Writing Practice: Your Technology Essay

  1. Outline Your Essay: Choose a specific technological advancement that you can explain and discuss its societal impact (e.g., the internet, mobile phones, social media, AI, renewable energy tech). Create a quick outline for a short expository essay (2-3 paragraphs) following the structure above.
  2. Draft Your Essay: Start writing your expository essay. Focus on:
  • Clearly explaining the technology and its impact.
  • Presenting a balanced view (if applicable).
  • Using precise vocabulary related to technology.
  • Organizing your ideas logically.
