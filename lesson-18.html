Part 2: Writing About Health

Your Writing Guides

When writing about health and well-being, your writing should be:

  1. Informative: Provide clear, accurate, and helpful information.
  2. Clear and Concise: Avoid jargon and get straight to the point.
  3. Encouraging: Motivate the reader to adopt healthy practices.
  4. Structured: Organize your information logically, often with headings or bullet points.

Writing Structure: The Informative Article on a Health Topic

This structure will help you write a clear and helpful informative article:

  • Title: Catchy and informative (e.g., "The Power of Sleep: Boosting Your Well-being").
  • Introduction (1 paragraph):
  • Hook: Start with a general statement about the importance of health or the specific health topic.
  • Background: Briefly introduce the health topic you will discuss (e.g., the role of sleep, benefits of exercise).
  • Thesis Statement (or controlling idea): State the main purpose of your article – what information will you provide?
  • Example Thesis: "Adequate sleep hygiene is a cornerstone of holistic health, profoundly impacting both physical and mental well-being by enhancing cognitive function and strengthening the immune system."

  • Body Paragraph 1: Explaining the Concept/Benefits (1 paragraph):
  • Topic Sentence: Introduce a key aspect or benefit of the health topic.
  • Elaboration: Explain how this aspect works or why it's beneficial, using specific details and vocabulary.
  • Example: "Quality sleep allows the body to repair and rejuvenate, directly boosting the immune system and supporting a healthy metabolism. It is during deep sleep that our bodies produce essential hormones for growth and recovery."
  • Body Paragraph 2: Practical Tips/Strategies (1 paragraph):
  • Topic Sentence: Provide practical advice or strategies for improving this aspect of health.
  • Elaboration: Offer actionable tips that readers can implement in their daily lives. Use clear, direct language.
  • Example: "To enhance your sleep hygiene, it's advisable to establish a consistent bedtime routine, avoid screens before bed, and ensure your sleeping environment is dark and quiet. Mindfulness exercises can also reduce stress before sleep."
  • Conclusion (1 paragraph):
  • Summarize: Briefly recap the main points and benefits discussed.
  • Reiterate Importance: Emphasize the overall significance of the health topic.
  • Final Encouragement: End with a motivating statement for the reader to prioritize their well-being.

Writing Practice: Your Health Article

  1. Outline Your Article: Choose a health and well-being topic that you find interesting and can provide information about (e.g., the benefits of a balanced diet, tips for stress management, the importance of hydration). Create a quick outline for a short informative article (2-3 paragraphs) following the structure above.
  2. Draft Your Article: Start writing your informative article. Focus on:
  • Providing clear and accurate information.
  • Using explanatory language and vocabulary.
  • Offering practical advice.
  • Maintaining an encouraging tone.
